Privacy Policy

Personal data

3D Print Randomizer had never collected and will never collect any personal data, browsing history etc.

In future 3D Print Randomizer may collect the browser version. This information is needed for decision on implementing new features, removing unused features or suggesting default settings for new users. It will happen only with your permission.

Third party services

3D Print Randomizer does not use any third party services.

The website

For statistical purposes 3D Print Randomizer counts only the link clicks.
